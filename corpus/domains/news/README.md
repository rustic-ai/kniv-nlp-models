## Characteristics

- Formal reporting style (who/what/when/where/why)
- Past tense narrative ("The company announced...")
- Named entities: people, organizations, locations, dates
- Quotations and attributions ("According to...", "X said...")
- Complex sentences with relative clauses
- Temporal expressions ("on Monday", "last quarter", "in 2024")
