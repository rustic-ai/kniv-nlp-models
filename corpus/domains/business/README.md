## Characteristics

- Formal financial prose (SEC filings: passive voice, long sentences, hedging language)
- Business communication (emails: imperative, scheduling, negotiation, reporting)
- Instructional/educational (textbooks: definitions, examples, explanations)
- Technical/procedural (ERP docs: workflows, module descriptions, configuration steps)
- Legal/contractual (CUAD: obligations, warranties, indemnification clauses)
- Academic (S2ORC: hypothesis-driven, citation-heavy, formal argumentation)
- Domain-specific vocabulary: accounts payable/receivable, bill of materials, lead time,
  EBITDA, revenue recognition, procurement, supply chain, inventory turnover, etc.
- Named entities: company names, people, locations, dates, monetary amounts, percentages
- Temporal expressions: fiscal quarters, reporting periods, effective dates
